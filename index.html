DA vs CDC: Which Approval Path Is Right for Your Project?

In Australia, the Development Application (DA) is the standard council approval path, offering flexibility for complex projects. In contrast, a Complying Development Certificate (CDC) is a faster, streamlined option for projects that meet strict, predefined standards. Understanding the difference is key to choosing the right strategy for your build.
